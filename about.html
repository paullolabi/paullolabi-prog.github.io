My Story

Hi, I'm Paul Olabi, a freelance web designer passionate about helping authors build professional websites that showcase their books and strengthen their online presence.

My interest in web design began with a fascination for how beautiful websites combine creativity, technology, and user experience. As I developed my skills, I realized that many talented authors needed websites that reflected the quality of their work.

Today I focus on creating clean, responsive websites, book landing pages, and digital experiences that help writers connect with readers and build lasting author brands.
